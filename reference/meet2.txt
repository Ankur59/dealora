● Coupon filtering uses average scoring per merchant and threshold-based selection for AI validation (12:02)
○ For each merchant, all coupons are scored and an average score is calculated
○ Only coupons scoring above a configurable threshold proceed to AI validation, reducing validation load
○ This prevents providers with fewer parameters from being unfairly penalized
● Retention of invalid coupons with labels rather than deletion to reduce redundant validations (01:07:56)
